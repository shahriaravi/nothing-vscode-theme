# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for everyone. Contributors are expected to show respect and kindness to others.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

Behaviors that are not tolerated:

- Harassment or discrimination in any form
- Personal attacks or derogatory comments

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers privately. We will review and address all concerns responsibly.
